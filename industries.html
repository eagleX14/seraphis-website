Cybersecurity Solutions by Industry

Every industry faces unique cyber risks, regulatory pressures, and operational constraints. Seraphis delivers sector-specific security strategies aligned to business outcomes, compliance obligations, and operational continuity.

Financial Services

Banks, insurers, and fintechs operate under constant threat from fraud, ransomware, and regulatory scrutiny.

  • • Key Risks: Account takeover, fraud, insider abuse
  • • Regulatory Drivers: POPIA, PCI-DSS, Basel-aligned controls
  • • Typical Attacks: Phishing, credential theft, ransomware
  • • Outcomes: Fraud reduction, audit readiness, zero downtime

Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals

Patient data sensitivity, legacy systems, and uptime requirements make healthcare a prime ransomware target.

  • • Key Risks: Data breaches, system outages
  • • Regulatory Drivers: POPIA, GDPR, data protection laws
  • • Typical Attacks: Ransomware, lateral movement
  • • Outcomes: Patient safety, data confidentiality, continuity

Retail & E-Commerce

High transaction volumes and customer data exposure require continuous monitoring and fraud detection.

  • • Key Risks: Payment fraud, data theft
  • • Regulatory Drivers: POPIA, PCI-DSS
  • • Typical Attacks: Web skimming, credential stuffing
  • • Outcomes: Customer trust, transaction integrity

Energy, Utilities & Mining

Cyber incidents in OT environments can cause physical damage, safety incidents, and national disruption.

  • • Key Risks: ICS compromise, operational shutdown
  • • Regulatory Drivers: Critical infrastructure regulations
  • • Typical Attacks: OT malware, remote access abuse
  • • Outcomes: Safety, uptime, infrastructure resilience

Telecommunications

Telecom operators face nation-state threats, service disruption risks, and massive data exposure.

  • • Key Risks: Network intrusion, data interception
  • • Regulatory Drivers: Data protection, lawful intercept
  • • Typical Attacks: Advanced persistent threats
  • • Outcomes: Network stability, customer trust

Government & Public Sector

Government systems are high-value targets for espionage, disruption, and data compromise.

  • • Key Risks: Espionage, service disruption
  • • Regulatory Drivers: National cybersecurity frameworks
  • • Typical Attacks: APTs, phishing campaigns
  • • Outcomes: Public trust, national resilience
