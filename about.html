Who We Are

Seraphis IT and Data Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a South Africa–based cybersecurity firm delivering enterprise-grade security, risk, and compliance services to organisations operating in highly regulated and high-risk environments.

We support enterprises, critical infrastructure operators, and public-sector organisations across Africa and internationally — providing security capabilities that are resilient, measurable, and aligned to global best practices.

Our approach is architecture-led, platform-native, and outcome-driven. We do not sell tools in isolation. We design, operate, and govern security as a business capability.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To secure enterprises, governments, and critical infrastructure against evolving cyber threats by delivering resilient, compliant, and intelligence-driven security solutions built on Microsoft platforms and global best practices.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global cybersecurity partner emerging from Africa — recognised for safeguarding digital trust, operational continuity, and national economic resilience.

Our Operating Principles

Security is a business risk
Cybersecurity decisions must support operational continuity, compliance, and strategic objectives.
Outcomes over tools
We measure success through reduced risk, faster detection, and improved resilience — not product deployment.
Ethics and confidentiality
Trust is foundational. We operate with strict confidentiality, professionalism, and regulatory awareness.
Resilience by design
Security must assume failure and focus on detection, response, and recovery.
