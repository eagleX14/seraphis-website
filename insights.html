Ransomware in South Africa: What Boards Need to Understand in 2026

Threat Intelligence • Ransomware • Board Risk
Ransomware remains the most disruptive cyber threat to South African enterprises. Boards must treat ransomware as a business continuity and governance issue — not an IT problem.
Read full analysis

Observed trends:

  • Double extortion (data theft + encryption) is now standard
  • Attackers target backups, Active Directory, and OT systems
  • Multi-million rand recovery costs are common

Board-level priorities:

  • Immutable, tested backups
  • Identity protection and network segmentation
  • Incident response retainers and tabletop exercises
  • Measured MTTD and MTTC KPIs

Business outcome: Reduced downtime, controlled recovery, and regulatory defensibility.
