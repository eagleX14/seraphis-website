Technology alone does not prevent breaches. Most successful cyber incidents exploit human behaviour, unclear decision-making, and untested response processes.

Seraphis delivers role-based, outcome-driven cybersecurity training designed to reduce risk exposure, improve executive confidence, and meet regulatory expectations — aligned to real attack patterns observed in South Africa, Africa, and globally.

Security Awareness Training (All Staff)

Designed to reduce phishing success rates, credential compromise, and social engineering incidents across the organisation.

  • Modern phishing and business email compromise tactics
  • Password hygiene and MFA awareness
  • Safe data handling and POPIA obligations
  • Remote work and mobile device risks
  • Incident reporting responsibilities
Outcome: Measurable reduction in phishing click rates and faster incident reporting.

Phishing & Social Engineering Simulations

Realistic phishing simulations tailored to industry, threat landscape, and regional context.

  • Email, SMS (smishing), and voice (vishing) simulations
  • Credential harvesting and malware delivery scenarios
  • Role-based targeting (finance, executives, IT)
  • Behavioural metrics and improvement tracking
Outcome: Demonstrable improvement in staff resilience and executive reporting metrics.

Executive & Board-Level Cyber Risk Workshops

Cybersecurity is now a board accountability issue. These workshops are designed for non-technical executives.

  • Understanding cyber risk in business terms
  • Legal and fiduciary responsibilities (POPIA, governance)
  • Ransomware decision-making under pressure
  • Cyber risk appetite and investment prioritisation
Outcome: Confident, informed executive decision-making during cyber crises.

Incident Response Tabletop Exercises

Simulated cyber incidents designed to test readiness, escalation paths, and communication under pressure.

  • Ransomware and data breach scenarios
  • Executive, legal, IT, and communications participation
  • Regulator and media pressure simulation
  • Post-exercise gap analysis and improvement plan
Outcome: Reduced response time, clearer ownership, and regulatory defensibility.

OT / ICS Cyber Hygiene Workshops

Tailored training for industrial environments where availability and safety are paramount.

  • IT vs OT threat differences
  • Common ICS attack paths
  • Secure remote access and vendor control
  • Joint IT/OT incident response planning
Outcome: Improved resilience without compromising operational safety.
